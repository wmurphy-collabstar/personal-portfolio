Future Projects

Below are some wireframes of projects I want to build and why.

Time to Relax...

Personally, I have a hard time relaxing, as I'm constantly moving from one task to another. There are many relaxing tools out there, so I thought it would be great to have one place on the internet where the point is to pause, be calm, and really try to relax.

The plan is to have a calm moving visual in the background, calming chime notifications, postiive quotes, stories being read out loud...

Cat Guide 101

I adopted a black cat two years ago, and her name is Raven. She is a lovely cat, but also a lot of responsibility. Since she is my first cat, I scrambled around all over the internet trying to find solution after solution for each problem I faced.

So the Cat Guide 101 will be a one-stop-shop for all things cat related, including food, litter box, exercise, health, and more.

Map of Memories

I have a lot of photos from different parts of my life, and times that I don't want to forget, but it is hard sifting through hundreds upon thousands of pictures and often I completely forget about them.

That is why I thought of memory mapping, where you write a sentence or two about a time you remember, and then can add photos for that topic. Each one of those topics would be connected in a graph style and then you can share your own map with friends and family, or others to help introduce you a bit.

Artistic Dreams

Like many people, I have many different lists of songs, artists, movies, books, etc. that I'm interested in and like to learn more about. For me it's very easy to make too many lists and forget what I wanted to look into.

That's why I would make a to-do list but of art you have never seen. And then to entice you to look into it, the website would play a preview of each item, like a snippet of a song, or the first page of a book, to entice you.
