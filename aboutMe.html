About me

I've been a Client Support Developer for 2 years at IBM and I've learned a lot from the experience. I've helped many customers really utilize the Quantum systems and been growing as a developer.

I want to use those experiences as a front end developer at a company who accepts me for who I am. What really interests me about this kind of development is that I get to be technical and creative at the same time. I've been trying all kinds of new designs, while honing my skills to program a website from scratch with frameworks.

I think software development is so inspiring. So much technology is used to help people in so many areas: health care, accessibility, and much more. I want to create websites that not only help the company I work at in the future, but I want to advocate for people with disabilities, minorities, those not represented.
